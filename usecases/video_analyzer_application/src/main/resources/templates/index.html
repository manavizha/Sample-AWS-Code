AWS Video Analyzer application

The AWS Video Analyzer sample application uses the Amazon Rekognition service and other AWS services, and the AWS SDK for Java version 2. Analyzing your videos in real-time has never been easier! Just perform these steps:

  1. Upload a video (.MP4) to an Amazon S3 bucket by choosing the Upload Video menu item.
  2. Choose Choose Video File and browse to a video located on your desktop.
  3. Choose Upload to upload your video to an Amazon S3 bucket.
  4. Choose Get Video to view the video located in the S3 bucket. Only 1 video can be in the bucket.
  5. Analyze the video and produce a report by choosing the Analyze Video menu item.
  6. Enter an email address in the email field and choose Analyze Video.
  7. Amazon SES is used to send an email with an Excel report to the specified email recipient.
